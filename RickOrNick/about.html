WHY?

Mostly for fun :)

When did you start going by Rick?
I first started going by Rick int he summer of 2011.
What is this whole Rick thing anyway?
Every summer from May 1st to September 1st I go by the name of Rick instead of Nick.
Where did you get the idea?
My dad. One summer while in college, he went by his middle name instead of his first name. I thought that was cool and wanted to do my own version of that :)
Do you really change your name?
Legally no. But I change my name for my email, social media, and intro myself as Rick everywhere I go. I am Rick in the summer!
